Designing for a Nonprofit Peer Connection App – Womenexus

September 2024 – June 2025

Designed user flows, interfaces, and a custom design system for a nonprofit community app that helps female-identifying students support each other through college and life transitions.

Project Overview

  • Team: 14 members – divided into back-end and front-end teams
  • Role: UI/UX Designer & Front-end Developer – led UX design and testing, UI creation, front-end implementation, and client communication
  • Tools: Figma, React Native, Gluestack UI
  • Goal: Partner with nonprofit organizations to support their software development needs. For Womenexus, we created a community-focused app to help female-identifying students navigate academics, careers, and life transitions through peer support.

Background & Context

Blueprint Boulder is a software development club on campus that partners with nonprofits to build meaningful, mission-aligned digital solutions. As volunteer designers and developers, we collaborate with community-focused organizations to create software that drives real impact.
